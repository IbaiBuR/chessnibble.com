Chessnibble is finally here!

Why chessnibble?

Well, since I was a child my two biggest hobbies
have been computers and chess.

When I was going to buy the domain I wanted
to find a name to combine both.

Since chessbits.com and chessbytes.com were
already bought I thought about
chessnibble.com.

The name stands for chess + nibble, and fits
the initial idea perfectly.
